Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Extra virgin olive oil and fresh garlic
  • Dried oregano, salt, and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook Spaghetti: Fill a large Dutch oven or pot with cold water 3/4 full and bring to a boil. Add 2 tsp salt and spaghetti.
  2. Ensure pasta does not stick together: Separate pasta with tongs a few times during the first 2 minutes of cooking to ensure it does not stick.
  3. Keep cooking uncovered for 5 more minutes or until al dente, stirring occasionally.
  4. Drain pasta: Test the spaghetti for doneness towards the end by grabbing one strand onto a plate with tongs. Do not overcook until too soft. The key to tasty pasta is cooked pasta with a bite. Drain in a colander.
  5. Cook garlic: Return the pot to medium heat and add the olive oil, 4 garlic cloves, and oregano. Cook for 20 seconds, stirring constantly. Garlic cooks fast and can easily burn, so donâ€™t walk away. Turn off the heat.
  6. Add remaining ingredients: Drained pasta, tomato sauce, remaining 1/2 tsp salt, ground black pepper, and 1 more fresh garlic clove. Stir gently until warmed through. That 1 clove of fresh garlic at the end is a must!
  7. Sprinkle with Parmesan... and serve this spaghetti with tomato sauce immediately!
